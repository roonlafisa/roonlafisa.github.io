About Drone Delivery Canada Corp

Drone Delivery Canada Corp is a developmental technology company focused on designing, developing, and implementing a commercially viable drone delivery system within the Canadian geography. Their goal is to provide retailers, service organizations, and government agencies with a just-in-time delivery service using drone technology. The company offers different service options, including Depot to Depot for rural applications and Depot to Consumer for logistics services directly from a retailer or warehouse to a consumer's home or business location.
