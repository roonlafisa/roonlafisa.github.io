About Direct Kinetic Solutions

Direct Kinetic Solutions is a developer of radioisotopic battery solutions for various applications including small satellites, asset tracking, and future-forward projects. Their power management system offers innovative beta-emitting radioisotope power sources that provide uninterrupted power over extended periods, eliminating the need for traditional lithium batteries and optimizing power and space efficiency.
