About Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company. It was founded by Elon Musk, JB Straubel, Martin Eberhard, Marc Tarpenning, and Ian Wright. Tesla designs, manufactures, and sells electric vehicles, solar energy products, and energy storage solutions.

Mission

Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. The company aims to create products that are both environmentally friendly and enjoyable to drive.

Innovation

Tesla has been at the forefront of innovation in the automotive industry, introducing advanced electric vehicles with cutting-edge features such as Autopilot, over-the-air software updates, and Supercharging stations.
