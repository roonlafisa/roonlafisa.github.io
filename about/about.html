Background

I was born and grew up in Bangladesh. I moved to Canada in 2014 to pursue my passion for RF systems and joined UBC for masters studies. Since defending my thesis in 2016, I have been working in the space industry to develop complex sub-systems. Progressively, I have developed more and more passion for space technology and innovation.

Timeline of Experience

I prefer to present my journey from a fresh college graduate to my current role as a hardware test engineering professional. Here is a timeline of what I learned, how I learned it, and where I learned it:
  • 2010-2013: Completed BSc from Islamic University of Technology in Bangladesh.
  • 2014-2016: Moved to Canada and completed MSc from the University of British Columbia. I am very grateful to mysupervisor Dr. Loic Markley his MERG research group for extraordinary mentorship.
  • 2017-2019: Worked at Helios Wire as the lead RF Engineer. Left the company after it was acquired by Echostar.
    • Moved to Vancouver from Kelowna.
    • Learnt RF system design for cubesats.
    • Learnt RF link budgetting.
    • Learnt practical antenna design for space.
    • Learnt about startup business challenges and practices.
  • 2019-2020: Worked at Zen Maker Lab as a robotics instructor. I taught robotics to kids aged 8-12.
    • Learnt prototyping simple electronics into product.
    • Realized how difficult the job of a teacher is.
  • 2019-2021: Worked for part-time as a consultant at Direct Kinetic Solutions .
    • Learnt about various applications of isotropic power
    • More practical link budgets.
    • Learnt about proposal writing for US DOD.
    • Learnt about cubesat EPS system design.
  • 2020-2021: Worked at SMT Research as an intermediate RF Engineer.
    • Learnt about construction iot for building health monitoring.
    • Picked up RFID battery less sensor.
    • Learnt hardware quality control for product release.
    • Hands-on manufacturing of sensors and data loggers.
    • Installed sensors in active construction site.
    • Learnt PCB ordering through cheap manufactrurers in China.
  • 2021-2023: Working at SpaceRyde as a Senior RF Specialist.
    • Moved to Toronto from Vancouver.
    • Advanced RF link budget calculations.
    • Assembly, integration and testing (AIT) of rocket avionics hardware.
    • Interfaced with ISED for RF regulatory licensing.
    • Picked-up solidworks CAD works.
    • Hands-on manufacturing of flight hardware.
    • Developed a few PCB boards from design to assembly - shout out to dear colleague Jonathan Colucci.

    • Attempted to implement environmental testing using MIL-810.
  • 2022: Picked up HTML, CSS and JS through building this website.
  • 2023: Got my basic drone pilot license for operating drones under 25 kg.
  • 2023: Worked at Drone Delivery Canada as a UAV System Integration Engineer.
    • Developed avionics hardware for an unmanned helicopter based on MOSA architecture.
    • Implemented system engineering practices following ARP 4754.
    • Received Safety Management Systems (SMS) training.
    • Learnt streaming camera videos via an LTE modem (microhard BulletCATX) on jetson based linux.
    • Worked on accessing Pixhawk 6c autopilot via Jetson Nano to monitor and control a drone.
