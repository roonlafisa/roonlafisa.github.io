An In-depth Look at IX Industrial Connectors

Introduction

Many users have experienced issues with the RJ45 connector due to its lack of robustness, including broken locking tabs and occasional contact problems. The IX Industrial connector provides a miniaturised, more reliable alternative to the RJ45. It conforms to the IEC 61076-3-124 standard and is manufactured by Harting.

Features of IX Industrial Connectors

The IX Industrial connector offers several important features:

  • Ethernet interface in the IP20 environment
  • Ethernet 6A performance for 1/10 Gbit/s Ethernet
  • 70% less installation space compared to typical RJ45 sockets
  • Continuous 360Â° shield design for high EMC immunity to interference
  • Available in two types: Type A (Cat6A Ethernet) and Type B (signal and bus communication)

Industrial Use in Harting's 8-Port Industrial Switch

Harting's 8-port ethernet switch, Ha-VIS eCon 2080GX-I-AW, utilizes IX Industrial connectors. Thanks to the 70% smaller size of these connectors, the switch is more compact, lightweight, and robust. Find out more about this switch on Harting's website and Newark.
