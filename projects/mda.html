My Role at MDA Space

Developing Test Stations for Satellite Constellation Program

At MDA, I am focused on the development of advanced test stations for a cutting-edge satellite constellation program. My role involves designing, implementing, and validating testing protocols for various satellite components to ensure their optimal performance and reliability in space.

In this capacity, I work closely with a multidisciplinary team to create and integrate test solutions that meet the stringent requirements of space missions. This includes:

  • Designing and developing automated test stations for satellite subsystems.
  • Conducting detailed performance analysis and reliability testing of satellite components.
