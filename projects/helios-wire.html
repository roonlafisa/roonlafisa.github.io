Helios Wire

About Helios Wire

Helios Wire was a developer of satellite-based IoT systems designed to assist in wireless satellite communications. The company's system connected to a machine-to-machine platform that tracked and improved wireless connectivity at low bandwidth and low service cost. They utilized a combination of low-Earth orbiting satellites and terrestrial networks for their global satellite-enabled system.

My Contributions

  • Designed, simulated, and deployed C, X, and S-Band antennas for CubeSat TT&C communication.
  • Conceptualized and modeled an IoT gateway to interface between Cubesat in space and IoT tags on the ground, conducting dynamic simulations using Solidworks.
  • Created and maintained invoices & POs, negotiated prices & terms of large-volume buying.
  • Generated drawings, technical specifications, and internal research papers for designed antennas to present key findings to management.

Achievements

During my time at Helios Wire, I made significant contributions to the development and deployment of satellite-based IoT systems. Some of my notable achievements include:

  • Successful design and deployment of C, X, and S-Band antennas for CubeSat TT&C communication, ensuring reliable and efficient communication in space.
  • Innovative conceptualization and modeling of an IoT gateway, bridging the communication between satellites and ground-based IoT devices, enhancing connectivity and data transfer capabilities.
  • Efficient management of purchasing processes, including invoice and PO maintenance, and negotiation of prices and terms for large-volume buying, optimizing procurement operations.
  • Creation of detailed technical drawings, specifications, and research papers, enabling effective communication of antenna designs and key findings to the management team.
